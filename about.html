Seethi Sahib Memmorial Polytechnic College
National Service Scheme Unit 131&172

National service scheme is an government-sponsored public service program under the ministry of youth affairs and sports, popularly known as NSS which was launched in 1969, focusing on the personality development of students through community service. The NSS units of Seethi Sahib Memorial Polytechnic College Tirur, have contributed many innovative technical projects to the society. Community service rendered by the two units [131&172] have taken several dimensions like,aid for the needy,reinstating damaged hospital equipments [Punarjjani],welfare work, philanthropy and charity.The units have also generously involved in fund raising for the pain and palliative patients. The NSS units in SSMPTC have also indulged in agricultural activities, thus creating a link between students and nature..
